Welcome to Zap Wrap

Fast, fresh, and flavorful!

Welcome to Zap Wrap! Get ready for a burst of flavor and speed in every bite. We’re all about fresh, tasty wraps and snacks made fast, just the way you like. Stop by and zap your hunger with something delicious!

Our signature wrap, the Zap Wrap Supreme, is a flavor-packed delight! Stuffed with tender grilled chicken, fresh crunchy veggies, melted cheese, and our secret zesty sauce, all wrapped in a warm tortilla—it’s a bite of pure bliss. Perfectly crafted for those who crave bold and delicious flavors!
Our ultimate burger, the Zap Burger Deluxe, is a mouthwatering masterpiece! Juicy, flame-grilled beef patty layered with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, fresh tomatoes, caramelized onions, and our signature smoky sauce, all nestled in a toasted bun. It’s the perfect blend of flavor and satisfaction in every bite!
Our golden, crispy fries are the perfect sidekick to any meal! Hand-cut, perfectly seasoned, and fried to crunchy perfection, they’re irresistibly delicious. Whether you dip them, share them, or savor them solo, our fries are a treat you’ll keep coming back for!
